About Me

My family and I moved to Houston from Louisiana in 2014.   For the last twenty years I have worked in the oil and gas industry.  Some of those jobs included Pump Supervisor, Slickline Operator, MWD Field Engineer, Real-Time Observation Consultant, and Wireline Engineer.

After several industry slow downs, I decided a career change was needed.  I began to pursue my Bachelor's in Computer Science at Western Governors University.   While attending classes I soon realized that in order for me to better understand the course work, I needed to know how to write code.   I then enrolled in DigitalCrafts Full Stack Immersive Bootcamp.  While at DigitalCrafts I learned many important skills in several programming languages that helped reinforce the course foundations in my bachelor's program.  Upon graduation from DigitalCrafts, I plan to finishing my Bachelor program at WGU before exploring the job market.
